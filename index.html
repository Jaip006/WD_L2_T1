KARAN AUJLA

Karan Aujla is an Indian singer, rapper, and songwriter in the Punjabi music industry. He has released over fifty-two singles as a lead artist and thirty-four as a lyricist. Also eleven of his singles as a lead artist have been featured on the global YouTube music chart, while nineteen have been featured on the UK Asian music chart.
